Tirana-Thailand-Tirana 10 days-1399€


  • DETAILED ITINERARY:
  • Day 1: Arrival in Bangkok
    • Flight: Departure from Tirana at 06:20 AM, arrival in Bangkok at 09:30 AM.
    • Accommodation: Check-in at a luxury hotel in Bangkok with breakfast included.
    • Evening: Free time to explore the local area or relax at the hotel.
  • Day 2: Bangkok City Tour
    • Visit the Grand Palace: A historic royal complex in Bangkok.
    • Explore Wat Phra Kaew: The Temple of the Emerald Buddha.
    • Visit Wat Arun: The Temple of Dawn, located on the banks of the Chao Phraya River.
    • Lunch at a traditional Thai restaurant.
    • Evening: Free time to visit the vibrant night markets in Bangkok.
  • Day 3: Day Trip to Ayutthaya
    • Visit the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Ayutthaya: Explore the ancient temples and ruins of the former capital of the Kingdom of Siam.
    • Lunch at a local restaurant in Ayutthaya.
    • Return to Bangkok in the evening for overnight stay.
  • Day 4: Travel to Chiang Mai
    • Flight: Depart from Bangkok to Chiang Mai.
    • Accommodation: Check-in at a luxury hotel in Chiang Mai with breakfast included.
    • Evening: Visit the famous Night Bazaar for shopping and local street food.
  • Day 5: Chiang Mai City Tour
    • Visit Doi Suthep Temple: A sacred site located on a mountain with breathtaking views over Chiang Mai.
    • Explore the old city temples and visit the local markets.
    • Lunch at a traditional Northern Thai restaurant.
    • Evening: Free time for leisure activities or enjoy a traditional Thai massage.
  • Day 6: Elephant Sanctuary Visit
    • Day trip to an ethical elephant sanctuary: Interact with rescued elephants in a sustainable environment.
    • Lunch at the sanctuary.
    • Return to Chiang Mai for an evening at leisure.
  • Day 7: Travel to Phuket
    • Flight: Depart from Chiang Mai to Phuket.
    • Accommodation: Check-in at a luxury hotel in Phuket with breakfast included.
    • Free time to relax at the beautiful beaches of Phuket.
  • Day 8: Phi Phi Islands Tour
    • Day trip to the Phi Phi Islands: Enjoy swimming, snorkeling, and sightseeing at one of Thailand’s most famous islands.
    • Lunch on the island.
    • Return to Phuket in the evening.
  • Day 9: Free Day in Phuket
    • Free day to explore the beaches, visit local markets, or relax at your leisure.
    • Optional activities: Spa treatments, boat tours, or local sightseeing tours.
  • Day 10: Departure
    • Check-out from the hotel and transfer to Phuket International Airport.
    • Flight: Departure from Phuket and return to Tirana.
  • PRICE AND CONCLUSIONS:
  • Price: 1,399 € per person.
  • Included in the price:
    • Round-trip flight from Tirana to Bangkok and back, with domestic flights within Thailand.
    • 9-night accommodation in luxury 4 or 5-star hotels in Bangkok, Chiang Mai, and Phuket, with breakfast included.
    • All transfers and transportation within Thailand as per the itinerary.
    • Guided city tours in Bangkok, Chiang Mai, and Phuket.
    • Excursions to Ayutthaya, Phi Phi Islands, and the Elephant Sanctuary.
    • Daily breakfast at the hotels.
    • Lunch during selected excursions and activities.
  • Important Notes:
    • Each traveler is allowed to carry one small bag up to 40 x 30 x 20 cm on the plane.
    • Checked-in luggage or larger baggage will have additional costs.
    • Some optional activities or attractions (such as spa treatments or additional excursions) may incur extra fees.
    • Visa arrangements are required for Albanian citizens; consult with the agency for details.
  • Child pricing: Children between 2 – 11 years old get a 300 € discount from the tour price.
