Tirana-New York City-Tirana 5 days-799€


    DETAILED ITINERARY:

    • Day 1: Arrival in New York
      • Flight: Departure from Tirana at 07:00 AM, arrival in New York at 11:00 AM.
      • Accommodation: Check-in at the hotel in New York.
      • Evening: Free time to explore Times Square and the surrounding areas.
    • Day 2: New York City Tour
      • Morning: A guided panoramic tour of Manhattan, including:
        • Statue of Liberty: Visit Liberty Island to see this iconic monument.
        • Central Park: A relaxing walk or optional bicycle tour through this famous park.
        • Empire State Building: Visit the observation deck for a stunning view of the city.
      • Afternoon: Free time for shopping and exploring neighborhoods such as Fifth Avenue or Greenwich Village.
    • Day 3: Museums and Cultural Attractions
      • Morning: Visit the Metropolitan Museum of Art or the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA), depending on your preference.
      • Afternoon: Take a boat tour along the Hudson River to see the skyline from the water.
      • Evening: Free time to explore the city at night or see a Broadway show.
    • Day 4: Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island
      • Morning: Take a ferry to Liberty Island to visit the Statue of Liberty, followed by a visit to Ellis Island and the Immigration Museum.
      • Afternoon: Free time for shopping or exploring other attractions in the city.
    • Day 5: Neighborhoods and Street Art
      • Morning: Explore famous neighborhoods like SoHo, Chinatown, and Little Italy.
      • Afternoon: Walk through the vibrant areas of Brooklyn, known for its street art and eclectic vibe.
      • Evening: Enjoy dinner at a local restaurant or café in one of New York's unique neighborhoods.
    • Day 6: Shopping and Free Time
      • Morning: Shopping at popular spots like Macy's Herald Square or the luxury stores on Fifth Avenue.
      • Afternoon: Free time to revisit any attractions or explore hidden gems of the city.
    • Day 7: Departure
      • Morning: Last minute shopping or a stroll in Central Park.
      • Flight: Departure from New York at 2:00 PM, arrival in Tirana at 7:00 AM the next day.
  • PRICE AND CONCLUSIONS:
    • Price: 799 € per person.
    • Included in the price:
      • Round-trip flight from Tirana to New York and back.
      • Accommodation in a 3 or 4-star hotel in New York for 6 nights.
      • Breakfast included for all days of the trip.
      • Guided city tour of Manhattan, including visits to major landmarks.
      • Boat tour along the Hudson River and visits to Liberty Island and Ellis Island.
      • Transfers and transportation within New York City during the trip.
      • All necessary entry tickets to museums and attractions as per the itinerary.
    • Additional Information:
      • Free time in the afternoons to explore the city at your leisure.
      • Optional activities like Broadway shows or extra shopping can be arranged at an additional cost.
    • Important Notes:
      • Price may vary depending on availability and travel dates.
      • For any optional activities or excursions, additional charges may apply.
      • It is recommended to check visa requirements for Albanian citizens before booking the trip.
  • Child pricing: Children between 2 – 11 years old get a 150 € discount from the tour price.
