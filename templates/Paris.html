Tirana-Paris-Tirana 3 days-349€


    DETAILED ITINERARY:

  • Day 1: Arrival in Paris
  • Flight: Departure from Tirana at 06:20 AM, arrival in Paris at 09:30 AM.

  • Panoramic Tour: Upon arrival, a panoramic tour of Paris will be conducted, where you will visit:
  • Champs-Élysées: One of the most famous and beautiful boulevards in Paris.
  • Petit Palais and Elysee Palace: The presidential palace of France.
  • Place de la Concorde: The largest square in Paris.
  • Pont Alexandre III: One of the most beautiful bridges in Paris, decorated with golden statues.
  • Marigny Theater and Le Pavillon Elysee: A famous 3-star Michelin restaurant in Paris.
  • Accommodation: Check-in at the hotel in Paris.
  • Free Evening: Free time for personal activities.
  • Day 2: Visit to Versailles and Paris
  • Breakfast: At the hotel.
  • Visit to Versailles: A tour of the Palace of Versailles, one of the most significant historical monuments in France.
  • Visit to Paris: After returning, you will visit:
  • Arc de Triomphe: One of the most famous monuments in Paris.
  • Place Trocadéro: A panoramic view of the Eiffel Tower.
  • Luxembourg Gardens: One of the most beautiful gardens in Paris.
  • Louvre Museum: One of the largest and most famous art museums in the world.
  • Eiffel Tower and Champ de Mars: Visit to the Eiffel Tower and a walk in Champ de Mars.
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral: One of the most famous cathedrals in Paris.
  • Optional: A boat trip on the Seine River for a beautiful view of Paris from the water (additional cost of 20 €).
    • Accommodation: Return and check-in at the hotel.
    • Free Evening: Free time for personal activities.
    • Day 3: Return to Tirana
    • Breakfast: At the hotel.
    • Transfer to Airport: Transfer to Beauvais airport for the return flight.
    • Flight: Departure from Paris at 17:00, arrival in Tirana at 19:30.

    • PRICE AND CONCLUSIONS:
    • Price: 349 € per person.
    • Included in the price:
    • Round-trip flight from Tirana to Paris and back.
    • Transfers and bus tours as per the program.
    • Accommodation in a 3 or 4-star hotel for 2 nights in Paris.
    • Breakfasts for all days of the trip.
    • A guide accompanying you during your stay outside Albania.
    • You are allowed to carry one small bag with a size of up to 40 x 30 x 20 cm in the plane.
    • (Note – any baggage larger than this size and checked-in luggage will have additional costs).

    • Not included in the price:
    • Everything not mentioned in the "Included in the price" section.
    • Lunches and dinners.
    • Entrance fees to monuments and museums.
    • Local tax of 7.80 € per person per night for 4* hotels and 5.20 € per person per night for 4* hotels
    • (mandatory and paid at the agency along with the payment for the tour).

    • Notes:
    • Meeting Point: From Tirana International Airport, you need to be at the airport at least 2 hours before the flight.
    • Child pricing: Children between 2 – 11 years old get a 40 € discount from the tour price.
