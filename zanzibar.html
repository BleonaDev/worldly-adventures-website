Tirana-Zanzibar-Tirana 7 days-1299€


    DETAILED ITINERARY:

  • Day 1: Arrival in Zanzibar
  • Flight: Departure from Tirana at 08:00 AM, arrival in Zanzibar at 04:00 PM.

  • Accommodation: Check-in at a 5-star hotel in Zanzibar.
  • Relax and enjoy the hotel facilities.

  • Day 2: Stone Town Tour
  • Morning: Breakfast at the hotel.
  • Visit to Stone Town: Explore the historical Stone Town, a UNESCO World Heritage site.
  • Highlights: House of Wonders, Sultan's Palace, and Darajani Market.

  • Day 3: Spice Tour
  • Morning: Breakfast at the hotel.
  • Tour: Visit a spice plantation and learn about Zanzibar's spice production.
  • Tasting: Enjoy spices such as cloves, nutmeg, and cinnamon.

  • Day 4: Prison Island & Dolphin Watching
  • Morning: Breakfast at the hotel.
  • Excursion: Take a boat trip to Prison Island and see the giant tortoises.
  • Afternoon: Dolphin watching and snorkeling.

  • Day 5: Safari Blue
  • Day trip: Full-day excursion to Safari Blue, including snorkeling and swimming in crystal-clear waters.
  • Lunch: Seafood barbecue on a private island.

  • Day 6: Relax and Explore Beaches
  • Morning: Free time to relax on the beach or explore the local area.
  • Optional: Enjoy water sports such as kayaking, jet skiing, or paddleboarding.

  • Day 7: Departure
  • Morning: Breakfast and check-out from the hotel.
  • Flight: Return flight to Tirana.

  • PRICE AND CONCLUSIONS:
  • Price: 1,199 € per person.
  • Included in the price:
    • Round-trip flight from Tirana to Zanzibar and back.
    • Accommodation in a 5-star hotel for 6 nights in Zanzibar.
    • Breakfasts for all days of the trip.
    • Transfers and boat trips as per the itinerary.
    • Excursions and tours as described (Stone Town, Spice Tour, Prison Island, Dolphin Watching, Safari Blue).
  • Additional Information:
    • Some activities may incur additional costs, such as optional water sports.
    • It is recommended to check visa requirements for Tanzanian entry for Albanian citizens.
    • Prices may vary depending on the season and availability.
  • Child pricing: Children between 2 – 11 years old get a 300 € discount from the tour price.
