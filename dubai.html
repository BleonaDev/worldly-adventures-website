Tirana-Dubai-Tirana 5 days-549€


    DETAILED ITINERARY:

  • Day 1: Arrival in Dubai
  • Flight: Departure from Tirana at 10:00 AM, arrival in Dubai at 6:00 PM.
  • Transfer: Transport from the airport to the hotel and check-in.
  • Free time: After check-in, free time to explore the area around the hotel or relax after the flight.
  • Day 2: Dubai City Tour
  • Visit to Burj Khalifa: Ascend to the "At The Top" observation deck for a panoramic view of the city.
  • Dubai Mall: Walk through the world's largest shopping mall, with opportunities for shopping and a visit to the Dubai Aquarium.
  • Burj Al Arab: Stop for photos near the iconic sail-shaped hotel.
  • Souk Madinat Jumeirah: Explore the traditional market with water canals and artisan shops.
  • Dubai Marina: Stroll along the marina with views of modern skyscrapers and luxury yachts.
  • Day 3: Desert Safari
  • Afternoon: Departure for a desert safari in 4x4 vehicles.
  • Activities: Dune bashing, sandboarding, and a chance to take photos of the sunset in the desert.
  • Bedouin Camp: Traditional BBQ dinner, oriental dance performances, and the opportunity to try traditional clothing or henna tattoos.
  • Return: Back to the hotel in the evening.
  • Day 4: Abu Dhabi - Capital of the UAE
  • Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque: Visit to the magnificent mosque, one of the largest in the world.
  • City Center: Walk along the Corniche, Abu Dhabi’s scenic waterfront boulevard.
  • Qasr Al Watan: Visit to the presidential palace to learn more about the country’s culture and history.
  • Ferrari World: Optional visit to the theme park for speed and sports car enthusiasts.
  • Return: Back to Dubai in the evening.
  • Day 5: Free Time and Departure
  • Free time: Last-minute shopping, visit to Dubai’s beaches, or other activities as preferred.
  • Check-out from the hotel and transfer to the airport.
  • Flight: Departure from Dubai at 8:00 PM, arrival in Tirana at 11:30 PM.

  • PRICE AND CONCLUSIONS:
  • Price: 549 € per person.
  • Included in the price:
    • Round-trip flight from Tirana to Dubai and back.
    • Transfers and transportation according to the itinerary.
    • 4-night accommodation in a 4-star hotel in Dubai.
    • Daily breakfast at the hotel.
    • Guided city tours as per the program, including visits to Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall, and Palm Jumeirah.
    • Excursion to the Desert Safari with dinner and entertainment.
    • A professional guide accompanying you throughout the trip.
  • Important Notes:
    • Each traveler is allowed to carry one small bag up to 40 x 30 x 20 cm on the plane.
    • Checked-in luggage or larger baggage will have additional costs.
    • Visa arrangements are required for Albanian citizens; consult with the agency for details.
  • Child pricing: Children between 2 – 11 years old get a 60 € discount from the tour price.
